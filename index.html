Pat's Salmon Cookies

Our Story

We are a family owned store that has been selling our cookies since 1915. Pat's grandmother had a cookie recipe that she would always make in the summer, called salmon cookies. Everyone in the family loved them, they would all be gon within seconds. When Pat's grandmother passed away, she left the cookie recipe to Pat. Thats when Pat decided that all of Seattle should try the cookies that he has loved since a kid.
